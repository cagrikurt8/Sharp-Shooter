Description

Our game is all about shooting, right? Let's add this feature to the game. The shooting process consists of two parts: aiming & firing The graphical object Gun Sight is responsible for aiming. In the previous stage, we have learned how we can draw it. Now, you need to make the Gun Sight move under player control.

Let's choose the arrow buttons to control the movement of the Gun Sight. Once players press an arrow key, the sight moves to a new location. Once the aiming is complete, players need to press the space button to fire a shot. As a result, the game must display the mark on the target Bullet Hole. All bullet holes should remain on the screen.

Each time the position of the Gun Sight changes or a new Bullet Hole appears, the Canvas must be updated as follows:

  1. Draw the Target on the Canvas as in the previous stage;
  2. Draw Bullet Holes on the Canvas;
  3. Draw the Gun Sight on the Canvas.

At the beginning of the program, you may create images for the Target, Gun Sight, and Bullet Hole and place them in separate BufferedImage variables. You can opt for drawImage in the predefined paintComponent method of Canvas.

Objectives

Implement the components and display them on the Canvas. The Target must satisfy the requirements of the previous stage. For testing purposes, the initial position of the Gun Sight must be at the center of Target. Be careful! The centers of those objects should coincide.

Implement the following keys to control the position of Gun Sight:

  • VK_UP moves it to the up by 10 pixels;
  • VK_DOWN moves it to the down by 10 pixels;
  • VK_LEFT moves it to the left by 10 pixels;
  • VK_RIGHT moves it to the right by 10 pixels.

The Gun Sight is limited by the screen. Due to testing reasons, limit the horizontal and vertical coordinates of the center of the Gun Sight in the range from 40 to 660.

A new Bullet Hole should appear every time players press the space bar. As in the previous stage, the color of bullets holes must be different from Color.BLACK, Color.WHITE, Color.DARK_GRAY.
