Description

The game that we will develop in this project can be described as follows:

  • There's a fixed target in the center of the playing field;
  • Players use the keyboard to move the gun sight to the center of the target;
  • Players can make a shot by aiming and pressing the fire button;
  • The closer the shot to the center of the target, the more points players get;
  • The goal of this game is to score as many points as possible for a limited number of attempts.

We will also add special effects to decrease the shooting precision.

In the first stage, we will create a game field and several graphical objects.

You need to construct three graphical objects:

  • Target is a family of concentric circles. The main target always stays still;
  • Gun Sight indicates the location of the sight;
  • Bullet Hole points a gunfire trace. They will appear once the shot has been fired.
Objectives

Draw the Target on the Canvas with the following requirements:

  • The center of the Target should be at (350, 350);
  • The Target should contain ten concentric circles with an increment in the radius of 30 pixels;
  • Use Color.BLACK and Color.WHITE as colors of the Target. Utilize them as shown in Example 1;
  • Use Color.DARK_GRAY as the Target background color;
  • Insert the Target into the Canvas without clipping.

Don't forget to draw a Bullet Hole on the Canvas with the center at (300, 500) in a color that is different from Color.BLACK, Color.WHITE, and Color.DARK_GRAY. You can define the size of a Bullet Hole as you'd like. As an option, it may be a circle with a radius of 5 pixels.

Draw the Gun Sight at your discretion. You can copy Example 1 if you want.
